How to Play the Game:

  • When you enter the game, you will get to choose a topic of your choice. Once you've selected a topic, press 'Enter' to start.
  • You will receive 5 questions based on the chosen topic. Each question will have 4 options to choose from.
  • Select the correct option from the given choices. If your answer is correct, your score will increase.
  • After answering all 5 questions, you can check your score and see which questions you answered incorrectly.
  • If you exit the game before answering all questions, your progress will not be saved.
  • If you press on NEXT button before selecting any option, it will be considered skipped.
